About Me

My name is Stanley, and I am from New York City. My interests lie with computers; I want to find out everything about them- from how they work to how people use them. My hobbies include reading, playing video games, watching movies, and hanging out with friends. I am currently double majoring in Information Security and Forensics and Applied Networking and Systems Administration.

Intro to Multimedia was actually a requirement for my Applied Networking and Systems Administration major. While I originally did not know what this course really was, it was a pleasant surprise to see that this course focuses on web design. Web design is something I have always wanted to delve into, but never had the time. I hope to learn a lot from the IMM course, and I hope I can apply what I learn in the future.
